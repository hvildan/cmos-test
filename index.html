Speech Quality Evaluation

Comparative Mean Opinion Score (CMOS) Test

Welcome!

Thank you for participating in this listening test. The goal of this study is to compare the naturalness and accent of two text-to-speech (TTS) systems. You will hear pairs of synthesized speech and judge which one sounds more natural and has less noticeable accent.

Please use headphones in a quiet environment for best results.

Instructions

  • You will hear two generated speech samples (A and B) for each trial
  • Listen to both samples before making your judgment
  • Rate which sample sounds more natural and has less accent on a -3 to +3 scale
  • You can replay audio as many times as you like
  • There are no right or wrong answers

Rating Scale

  • -3: B is much better than A
  • -2: B is better than A
  • -1: B is slightly better than A
  •  0: A and B are about the same
  • +1: A is slightly better than B
  • +2: A is better than B
  • +3: A is much better than B
